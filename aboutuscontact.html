Our Story

We believe the smartest home is the one you don’t notice. The home that knows exactly how you live. The home that does everything you want without you saying so (and those few times you do want to say so, you’ll be able to; whether you’re laying in bed or on the beach.)

How We’re Different

Instead of focusing on just the lighting, or just the thermostat, or just the TV, or just etc; we offer the full package. After all, your home isn’t just made up of a couple lights; its home because of the way everything comes together. You’ve spent a lot of time making it “home”, so its important to us to ensure it stays that way. Everyone is different, everyone’s home is different; so we don’t just provide some generic one-size fits all package and let you figure out the rest. We’ll configure everything to work together based on your unique lifestyle.
