  • Lighting

    Want to control all your lights with a single command? Want random lights to turn on & off when you're away? No more running around the house to turn everything off.

  • Heating & Cooling

    Adjust the thermostat from anywhere in the world whether you're at work or on vacation so it's always the perfect temperature when you're home; and saving you money when you're not.

  • Security

    Hate having to look for the keys every time you go out? Don't worry, your door will unlock as you're pulling into the driveway.

  • Home Entertainment

    Was it HDMI 2 or 5? Gone are the days of having a separate remote for everything. One click and your TV and sound system will turn on to the exact channel or movie you want.

  • Voice Control

    No need to walk up to the light switch or the thermostat anymore. Just say what you want.

  • Sensors

    Sensors: the backbone of automation. Know exactly when you & your family came & went. Know when something opens or closes. Know when there's people over when there shouldn't be.
